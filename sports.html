Sports Data

This page contains sports data visualisations from a variety of sources

This chart takes a look at annual household spending on sporting services and recreation from 2005 to 2019 in the UK. There is a clear increase in spending from 2012 onwards which could be attributed to the home Olympics. The data was taken from the ONS and manually entered into json objects instead of imported through a URL.

This chart shows the percentage of people that participate in aerobic and muscle strengthening physical activity accross EU countires in 2014. The data was taken from Eurostat and hosted via Github in a csv file.

This chart shows the average participation in aerobic and muscle strengthening physical activity in the EU by level of eduational attainment. This chart uses the same Eurostat data as the chart above. However, I have constructed an average participation level across all EU countries for each level of educational attainment.
